      ✦ Understanding Personal Years

      How the Personal Year Cycle Works

      Your Personal Year shifts on your birthday each year — not January 1st. From your birthday in one year to the day before your next birthday, you are in a specific Personal Year energy that runs in a 9-year cycle (plus Master Number years of 11 and 22).

      Formula: Birth Day + Birth Month + Your Active Year. Reduce the total to a single digit. Stop if you reach 11 or 22 (Master Numbers). The "active year" is the calendar year your current Personal Year began (on your last birthday).

      Example: Born 16th March → 16 + 3 + 2026 = 2045 → 2+0+4+5 = 11 (Master Number — do not reduce further). Personal Year 11 for 2026.

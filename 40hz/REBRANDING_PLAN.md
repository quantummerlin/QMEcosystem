## Visual Design Strategy

### Color Scheme Evolution
Keep the sophisticated purple/gold base but adjust for scientific positioning:

**Primary Colors:**
- Deep Purple: `#1a0033` → `#0f0f23` (Darker, more scientific)
- Violet: `#8b5cf6` → `#7c3aed` (Keep, slightly more vibrant)
- Gold: `#fbbf24` → `#f59e0b` (Slightly more amber for energy)

**New Accent Colors:**
- Cyan/Turquoise: `#06b6d4` (Represents gamma waves/technology)
- Electric Blue: `#3b82f6` (Cognitive enhancement)
- Soft Green: `#10b981` (Brain health/natural)

### Typography
- **Headings**: Modern sans-serif (keep current system fonts)
- **Body**: Clean, highly readable
- **Numbers**: Monospace for frequencies to emphasize precision

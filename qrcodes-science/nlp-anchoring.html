What is NLP Anchoring?

Neuro-Linguistic Programming (NLP) Anchoring is a technique where a specific stimulus (visual, auditory, or kinesthetic) is paired with a desired emotional or mental state. Once the association is established through repetition, triggering the stimulus automatically activates the state.

Think of it like Pavlov's dogs, but applied to human consciousness. When you hear your favorite song, you're instantly transported back to the memory and emotion associated with it. That's anchoring‚Äîand the associated state is automatically activated.

What Is Google Gemini & How to Use It

Google's AI assistant ‚Äî and why it might become your favourite tool

üìñ 7 min read üìÖ February 2026 üéØ Beginner

Google Gemini is Google's answer to ChatGPT ‚Äî but it's not just a copycat. It has some genuinely unique strengths, especially if you use other Google products. Here's everything you need to know to get started.

What Google Gemini Actually Is

Gemini is Google's AI chatbot, available for free at gemini.google.com. Like ChatGPT, you type questions or instructions and get back detailed responses.

But Gemini has a few tricks that set it apart:

  • Built by Google: It's trained on a massive amount of web data and integrates with Google Search, Maps, YouTube, and more
  • Multimodal: It can understand images, not just text ‚Äî upload a photo and ask about it
  • Google ecosystem: Works seamlessly with Gmail, Docs, and Drive if you use Google Workspace
  • Always up-to-date: The free version can access current web information

How to Get Started (3 Steps)

1

Go to gemini.google.com

Sign in with any Google account (Gmail). If you have a Google account, you already have access. No separate signup needed.

2

Ask your first question

Try something like: "What's happening in the world of AI this week? Summarise the top 3 developments."

3

Try an image upload

Click the image icon and upload a photo. Ask: "Describe what you see in this image" or "What plant is this?"

5 Things Gemini Does Better Than You'd Expect

1. Research with sources

Ask Gemini a factual question and it often shows links to its sources. This makes it easier to verify information ‚Äî something ChatGPT doesn't do as well on the free tier.

Try this prompt
What are the current scientific theories about why people have different personality types? Include sources.

2. Summarise YouTube videos

Paste a YouTube URL into Gemini and ask for a summary. It can pull key points from the video without you watching the whole thing.

Try this prompt
Summarise the key points from this YouTube video in 5 bullet points: [paste YouTube URL]

3. Analyse images

Upload a screenshot of your Soul Blueprint reading and ask Gemini to explain it, find patterns, or suggest focus areas.

4. Compare things side by side

Gemini is excellent at structured comparisons ‚Äî products, ideas, personality traits, career paths.

Try this prompt
Compare the personality traits of someone with a Cancer Sun vs. a Capricorn Sun. Format as a side-by-side table with categories: emotional style, work approach, relationship needs, and growth areas.

5. Export to Google Docs

Click "Export to Docs" on any Gemini response to save it directly to your Google Drive. Great for building a personal insights journal over time.

Gemini + Soul Blueprint

  1. Generate your free Soul Blueprint reading

üí° Power move: Run the same reading through both ChatGPT and Gemini. You'll get different perspectives ‚Äî ChatGPT tends to be more poetic, Gemini more analytical. Together they give you a fuller picture.

Quick Summary

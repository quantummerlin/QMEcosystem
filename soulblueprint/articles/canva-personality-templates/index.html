Common Mistakes to Avoid

  1. Using Pro templates on a free account. Look for the "Free" badge in Canva's template browser. Pro templates have a crown icon ‚Äî you can still preview them but can't export without watermarks.
  2. Not customizing colors. The default template colors may clash with your personality reading's aesthetic. Always update to the Soul Blueprint palette (#667eea ‚Üí #764ba2) or your AI-suggested colors.
  3. Forcing content into the wrong template. A 10-point reading doesn't fit in a template with 3 text boxes. Match content volume to template capacity. Use the AI adapter prompt to resize.
  4. Ignoring mobile preview. Most people will see your content on their phone. In Canva, zoom out to see how it looks at thumbnail size. If text is unreadable, increase font size.
  5. Skipping the AI formatting step. Raw reading text is too long and unstructured for most templates. Always run it through AI first to extract and format the right pieces.

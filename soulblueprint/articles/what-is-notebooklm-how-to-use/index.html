What NotebookLM Does

NotebookLM is a free AI tool by Google that works differently from ChatGPT or Gemini. Instead of having an open conversation, you feed it your own documents ‚Äî and it becomes an AI expert on whatever you give it.

Here's what makes it special:

  • Source-grounded: It only uses the information YOU provide ‚Äî no hallucinations from random internet data
  • Audio Overview: It generates a realistic two-person podcast discussing your documents
  • Smart summaries: Upload a 50-page document and get a perfect summary in seconds
  • Q&A mode: Ask questions and it answers specifically from your sources
  • Free: No subscription needed ‚Äî just a Google account
üí° Why this matters for you: Upload your Soul Blueprint reading and NotebookLM will generate a podcast where two AI hosts discuss YOUR personality in detail. It's genuinely mind-blowing the first time you hear it.

How to Use NotebookLM (Step by Step)

1

Go to NotebookLM

Visit notebooklm.google.com and sign in with your Google account.

2

Create a new notebook

Click "New Notebook". Give it a name like "My Soul Blueprint Reading".

3

Add your source

Click "Add source" and choose "Copied text". Paste your entire Soul Blueprint reading. You can also upload PDFs, Google Docs, or website URLs.

4

Generate an Audio Overview

Click the "Audio Overview" button. NotebookLM will create a podcast-style conversation between two AI hosts discussing your reading. This takes 1-3 minutes to generate.

5

Ask follow-up questions

Use the chat panel to ask questions about your reading. NotebookLM answers specifically from your source material.

What the Audio Overview Sounds Like

The Audio Overview feature is NotebookLM's killer feature. Here's what to expect:

  • Two natural-sounding AI voices discuss your reading like a podcast
  • They highlight the most interesting patterns and contradictions
  • They explain complex concepts (like Saturn Return or North Node) in plain language
  • The conversation typically lasts 5-15 minutes
  • You can download the audio as an MP3

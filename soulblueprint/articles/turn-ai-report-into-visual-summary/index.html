What a Visual Summary Looks Like

A personality visual summary is a single-page graphic that shows:

  • Your core placements (Sun, Moon, Rising) with symbols
  • Your top 3 strengths and 3 growth areas
  • Your key numbers (Life Path, Expression, Soul Urge)
  • A one-line "soul statement" that captures your essence
  • Your element balance and energy profile

Think of it as a personality ID card â€” everything important at a glance.

Step 1: Extract Your Key Data

First, ask AI to pull the most visual-friendly data from your reading:

Data extraction prompt
I'm going to share my personality reading. Please extract the following data in a clean, structured format: 1) Sun sign, Moon sign, Rising sign (with emoji symbols), 2) Top 3 personality strengths (2-3 words each), 3) Top 3 growth areas (2-3 words each), 4) Dominant element (fire/earth/air/water), 5) Life Path number and its meaning in one sentence, 6) A one-sentence 'soul statement' that captures my essence, 7) My personality in exactly 3 words. Here's my reading: [paste reading]

Step 2: Generate a Layout Description

Now ask AI to design a visual layout you can recreate:

Layout design prompt
Using the data you extracted, design a visual personality card layout. Describe it as if you're giving instructions to a graphic designer. Include: exact placement of each element, colour palette (use cosmic/celestial theme with my dominant element's colours), typography hierarchy, and decorative elements. The final size should be 1080x1080px for Instagram or 1000x1500px for Pinterest.

Step 3: Build It in Canva (Free)

Take your AI-designed layout and bring it to life:

  1. Open Canva (free account works)
  2. Create a new design (1080x1080 for Instagram, 1000x1500 for Pinterest)
  3. Set the background to a dark celestial gradient
  4. Add your core placements at the top with zodiac symbols
  5. Create a strengths section with icons
  6. Add your soul statement as the centrepiece
  7. Include your numerology data at the bottom
  8. Add "Generated by Soul Blueprint âœ¨" as a subtle watermark

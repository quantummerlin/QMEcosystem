Design a Side-by-Side Infographic

Open Canva and create a side-by-side layout that puts both partners' key placements next to each other:

  • Size: A3 landscape (16.5 √ó 11.7 in) works best for side-by-side layouts. A4 landscape is a good compact option.
  • Layout: Divide the poster into three columns ‚Äî Partner A on the left, shared/compatibility data in the centre, Partner B on the right
  • Left & right columns: Each person's name, Sun/Moon/Rising, top 3 strengths, dominant element, Life Path Number
  • Centre column: Shared strengths, relationship superpower, elemental balance, love language match
  • Colour palette: Use each partner's element colour on their side (Fire = warm reds/oranges, Water = teals/blues, Earth = greens/browns, Air = lavenders/silvers) with a blended gradient in the centre

Search Canva for "comparison infographic" or "vs poster" templates as starting points. Replace the placeholder content with your compatibility data.

üí° The centre column is the magic The individual sides show who each person is. But the centre column ‚Äî where you list their shared strengths, relationship superpower, and what makes them work ‚Äî is what makes this gift extraordinary. Make the centre column visually prominent with a slightly different background or a subtle heart/infinity symbol.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  1. Including relationship challenges or conflicts. This is a gift, not couples counselling. Focus on what makes them great together. Remove any language about tension, friction, or "things to work on."
  2. Getting one partner's birth data wrong. Double-check both birth dates (and times if available). A compatibility report based on incorrect data is embarrassing and misleading.
  3. Making the design too busy. A side-by-side infographic has a lot of data. Use whitespace, clear sections, and hierarchy to keep it readable. Less is more ‚Äî pick the top highlights rather than cramming everything in.
  4. Forgetting to explain the gift. Include a note explaining that you generated both their cosmic blueprints and used AI to discover their compatibility. Without context, the gift might confuse them.
  5. Only focusing on one partner. Give equal space and attention to both people. If one person's section looks richer or more flattering, rebalance it. Both partners should feel equally celebrated.

What ChatGPT Actually Is

ChatGPT is a free AI chatbot made by OpenAI. You type something in plain English (or almost any language), and it writes back a helpful response.

Think of it as a conversation partner that:

  • Has read billions of pages of text
  • Can write, summarise, explain, brainstorm, and translate
  • Responds in seconds
  • Is available 24/7 at chat.openai.com
💡 Key thing to know: ChatGPT doesn't search the internet in real time (unless you're on the Plus plan with browsing enabled). It generates responses based on patterns it learned during training.

How to Get Started (3 Steps)

1

Create a free account

Go to chat.openai.com and sign up with your email, Google, or Apple account. No credit card needed.

2

Type your first message

In the text box at the bottom, type anything. Try: "Explain what you can do for me in 3 bullet points."

3

Have a conversation

You can reply to keep the conversation going. Say things like "make it shorter", "give me an example", or "now do it for a different topic".

10 Things You Can Do With ChatGPT Today

Here are 10 practical things to try right now — copy and paste these straight into ChatGPT:

1. Explain something complex
Explain quantum computing to me like I'm 12 years old.
2. Write an email
Write a polite email to my landlord asking for an extension on this month's rent. Keep it under 100 words.
3. Brainstorm ideas
Give me 10 creative date night ideas that cost under $20.
4. Summarise something long
Summarise the main ideas of this text in 5 bullet points: [paste your text here]
5. Get a recipe
I have chicken, rice, broccoli, and soy sauce. Give me a quick dinner recipe with these ingredients.
6. Plan a trip
Plan a 3-day weekend trip to Lisbon for two people on a budget. Include must-see spots and food recommendations.
7. Learn a new concept
Teach me the basics of investing in index funds. Assume I know nothing about finance.
8. Improve your writing
Rewrite this paragraph to sound more professional and confident: [paste your text here]
9. Self-discovery prompt
I'm a Virgo Sun, Pisces Moon, Scorpio Rising. Based on these placements, what are my likely strengths and blind spots in relationships? Give me practical advice.
10. Create a study plan
Create a 30-day study plan for learning Spanish from scratch. I can dedicate 20 minutes per day.

Tips for Getting Better Results

  • Be specific: "Write a 200-word bio for a freelance designer" beats "Write a bio"
  • Give context: Tell ChatGPT who it's writing for, what tone you want, and what format
  • Iterate: Say "make it shorter", "add humor", or "now make a version for Instagram"
  • Use follow-ups: ChatGPT remembers your conversation — build on previous messages
⚠️ What ChatGPT can't do: It can make mistakes (called "hallucinations"), doesn't know real-time events (on the free plan), and shouldn't be used for medical, legal, or financial advice without verification.

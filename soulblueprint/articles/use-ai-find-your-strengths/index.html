Use AI to Find Your Hidden Strengths

A guided exercise to discover talents you didn't know you had

üìñ 8 min readüìÖ February 2026üéØ Beginner

You probably know your obvious strengths. But your personality reading contains clues to strengths you might not recognise ‚Äî abilities that feel so natural you don't think of them as special. This guided exercise helps you find them.

Why Hidden Strengths Matter

Research in positive psychology shows that people who use their signature strengths daily are significantly happier and more productive. But most people can only name 2-3 of their strengths. Your personality reading likely reveals 8-10.

The "hidden" ones are often your most powerful ‚Äî because they come so naturally you don't even recognise them as strengths.

The 5-Step Strengths Discovery Exercise

Step 1: Extract Your Obvious Strengths

Obvious strengths prompt
Based on this personality reading, identify my 5 most obvious strengths ‚Äî the ones that are clearly stated or strongly implied. For each, explain: 1) What it is in plain language, 2) How it probably shows up in my daily life, 3) A situation where this strength really shines. Here's my reading: [paste reading]

Step 2: Uncover Your Hidden Strengths

Hidden strengths prompt
Now look deeper. Based on the COMBINATION of traits in my reading (not individual placements), what strengths emerge that aren't immediately obvious? These are strengths that: - Come from the interaction of two or more traits - Might feel so natural to me that I don't consider them special - Other personality types would find difficult. Identify 5 hidden strengths I might not recognise in myself.

Step 3: Validate With Evidence

This is the crucial step most people skip. For each strength AI identified, ask yourself:

  • Can I think of a specific time this showed up in my life?
  • Have other people ever commented on this ability?
  • Does it feel easy and natural to me (even if others struggle with it)?

If you answer yes to at least 2 of 3, it's likely a real strength.

Strengths map prompt

Step 5: Create an Application Plan

Application plan prompt
Based on my strengths map, create a practical plan for using my top 3 strengths more intentionally. For each strength: 1) One way to use it at work this week, 2) One way to use it in relationships, 3) One way to use it for personal growth, 4) A warning sign that I'm overusing this strength (every strength has a shadow side).

Exercise Summary

  1. Extract 5 obvious strengths from your reading
  2. Uncover 5 hidden strengths from trait combinations
  3. Validate each with personal evidence
  4. Map them across 5 life categories
  5. Apply your top 3 with a weekly action plan

Total time: ~30 minutes. Impact: potentially life-changing.

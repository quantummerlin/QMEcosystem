Step-by-Step Career Analysis

Understand Which Placements Drive Career

Not every part of your reading is career-relevant. Focus on these key sections:

  • Midheaven (MC): Your public reputation, career ambitions, and the legacy you want to build. This is the single most career-relevant placement.
  • Sun Sign: Your core identity and what work makes you feel alive. Fire signs need excitement; Earth signs need tangible results.
  • 6th House: Your daily work style ‚Äî routines, health, service orientation. This reveals whether you need structure or freedom in your day-to-day.
  • 10th House: Career, authority, and public standing. Overlaps with the Midheaven but includes deeper themes about how you handle responsibility.
  • North Node: Your soul's growth direction. Career aligned with your North Node feels challenging but deeply fulfilling.
  • Saturn: Where you face your toughest lessons ‚Äî and where mastery awaits if you do the work.
  • Elemental Balance: Fire-heavy readings suit entrepreneurial roles. Water-heavy readings thrive in healing, creative, and people-centered work.

Copy all of these sections from your reading. The more career-relevant data you give AI, the more specific the output.

Identify Your Ideal Work Environment

Career happiness isn't just about what you do ‚Äî it's about where and how you do it. Your reading reveals your environmental needs:

  • Fire-dominant readings: Need fast pace, visible impact, competition, and room to lead. Open floor plans and startup energy suit you.
  • Earth-dominant readings: Need stability, clear metrics, tangible output, and steady progression. Structured organizations with defined growth paths.
  • Air-dominant readings: Need intellectual stimulation, variety, collaboration, and communication-heavy roles. Think tanks, agencies, and cross-functional teams.
  • Water-dominant readings: Need emotional safety, meaningful mission, creative freedom, and human connection. Nonprofits, therapy, arts, and purpose-driven organizations.

Your Moon sign especially reveals what you need to feel emotionally safe at work ‚Äî which is more important than most career advice acknowledges.

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  1. Only using your Sun sign for career guidance. Your Midheaven, 6th house, 10th house, and North Node are far more career-specific than your Sun sign alone.
  2. Taking AI career suggestions as absolutes. AI maps patterns ‚Äî it doesn't know your bills, your family situation, or your local job market. Use it as a compass, not a GPS.
  3. Ignoring the South Node trap. The most comfortable career path is often the one your South Node has mastered already. Growth lives in the North Node direction.
  4. Skipping the environment analysis. The right job in the wrong environment still feels wrong. Your Moon and elemental balance reveal what you need around you to thrive.
  5. Not revisiting annually. Your career needs evolve. Re-run this analysis yearly to see how your priorities and growth direction have shifted.

The 3-Tier AI Journaling System

Tier 1: Daily Check-In (2 minutes)

Start each day (or end it) with this quick prompt. Save it in your notes app with your personality context pre-filled:

Daily check-in template
Quick personality-aware check-in. I'm a [Sun/Moon/Rising]. Today I felt: [one word]. The strongest emotion was: [describe]. My energy level: [1-10]. Based on my personality type, what might be driving this feeling? Ask me one follow-up question to help me reflect deeper.

Tier 2: Weekly Reflection (10 minutes)

Every Sunday, do a deeper review:

Weekly reflection template
Weekly reflection for a [Sun/Moon/Rising]. This week: Best moment: [describe]. Hardest moment: [describe]. Something I avoided: [describe]. Something I'm proud of: [describe]. Based on my personality patterns, what themes do you notice? Which of my natural tendencies helped me this week, and which ones held me back? Suggest a specific focus for next week.

Tier 3: Monthly Deep Dive (20 minutes)

Once a month, share multiple entries with AI for pattern analysis:

Monthly pattern analysis
Here are my journal entries from the past month. My personality profile for context: [paste reading or key placements]. Analyse these entries and tell me: 1) What emotional patterns repeat? 2) What situations consistently trigger stress for me? 3) What am I getting better at? 4) What am I still avoiding? 5) What growth do you see that I might not recognise? 6) One powerful question I should sit with this month. 7 Personality-Specific Journal Prompts

Use these when your regular prompts feel stale:

For overthinkers (Air signs / Virgo)
I've been overthinking about [topic]. Help me get out of my head. Based on my personality type, what's my body probably trying to tell me that my mind won't let through? Give me a 2-minute physical grounding exercise.
For people-pleasers (Libra / Cancer / Pisces)
I said yes to something I wanted to say no to again. Based on my personality reading, why do I do this? What am I actually afraid of? Write me a kind but firm script I could use next time to set a boundary.
For perfectionists (Virgo / Capricorn)
I'm being too hard on myself about [situation]. My inner critic is loud today. Based on my chart, why is my perfectionism activated? Write a message from the compassionate part of my personality to the critical part.
For the restless (Sagittarius / Gemini / Aries)
I feel stuck and bored. Nothing excites me right now. Based on my personality, is this a sign I need change, or a sign I need to go deeper where I am? What would each look like specifically?

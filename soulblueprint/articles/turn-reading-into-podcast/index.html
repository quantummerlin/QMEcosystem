Common Mistakes to Avoid

  1. Too little source material. If you only paste one short reading section, the audio will be brief and shallow. Aim for at least 3‚Äì5 sections (1,000+ words) for a rich discussion.
  2. Not labeling the source clearly. Add a title and context at the top of your pasted text so the AI hosts know who they're discussing and why.
  3. Refreshing during generation. The audio takes 2‚Äì5 minutes to generate. Refreshing the page will cancel it and you'll have to start over.
  4. Expecting to edit the audio. NotebookLM generates a complete episode ‚Äî you can't edit individual sections. If you don't like the result, regenerate for a different version.
  5. Forgetting to download. Audio Overviews are stored in your notebook, but downloading gives you a permanent copy you can share anywhere.

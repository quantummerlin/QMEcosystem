# Article Visual Guide — Soul Blueprint

## Purpose

This is the definitive reference for every image needed across all 70 Soul Blueprint articles. Each article gets three image descriptions:

1. **Hero Image** (1200×675) — the main visual that sets the mood, used in-article above the fold
2. **OG/Social Card** (1200×630) — what people see when the article is shared on social media
3. **Pinterest Pin** (1000×1500) — vertical format optimised for Pinterest saves and clicks

Plus **In-Article Screenshot Notes** describing key tutorial moments that need visual documentation.

All descriptions are **tool-agnostic** — use them with AI image generators, Canva, Figma, or manual design.

---

## Brand Visual Style Guide

### Palette

| Element | Value |
|---------|-------|
| Primary gradient | `#667eea` → `#764ba2` (blue-violet to purple) |
| Background | White (`#ffffff`) or soft gray (`#fafafa`) |
| Text | Dark navy (`#1a1a2e`) or charcoal (`#2d2d2d`) |
| Accent | Pillar-specific color (see below) |

### Pillar Colors

| Pillar | Accent Color | Hex |
|--------|-------------|-----|
| Getting Started | Electric Blue | `#0984E3` |
| Self-Discovery | Soft Lavender | `#A29BFE` |
| Create Visuals | Deep Purple | `#6C5CE7` |
| Audio & Stories | Warm Orange | `#E17055` |
| Share & Grow | Teal | `#00CEC9` |
| Go Deeper | Gold | `#F39C12` |
| Gift & Print | Hot Pink | `#E84393` |

### Typography

- **Titles on images:** Playfair Display Bold, 48-72pt
- **Subtitles/body on images:** Inter Semi-Bold, 24-36pt
- **Always:** High contrast (dark text on light, or white text on dark/gradient)
- **Readable at thumbnail size** — if you can't read the title in a 200px-wide preview, the text is too small

### Mood & Style

- **Luminous and cosmic** — soft glows, gradient backgrounds, celestial elements (stars, constellations, soft light)
- **Personal and warm** — not corporate, not clinical. Think handwritten meets high-tech
- **Clean negative space** — one focal element per image, breathing room around text
- **Brand mark:** Small "Soul Blueprint · Quantum Merlin" in bottom-right, semi-transparent white or dark depending on background

### Do NOT

- Use stock photo people with fake smiles
- Use busy, cluttered compositions with too many elements
- Use neon or harsh colors that clash with the pastel-cosmic brand
- Add "FREE GUIDE" badges or price callouts
- Use generic AI-art that doesn't relate to the article topic

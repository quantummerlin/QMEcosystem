## Risk Assessment & Mitigation

### Revenue Risks

**Ad Blocker Usage (30-40% of users):**
- Mitigation: Focus on affiliate revenue (not blocked)
- Diversify monetization streams

**Seasonal Traffic Fluctuations:**
- K-pop comebacks = traffic spikes
- Summer slump (school break)
- Mitigation: Content calendar aligned with K-pop releases

**Competition:**
- Other astrology/K-pop tools emerge
- Mitigation: Build strong brand, community, unique features

### Traffic Risks

**Algorithm Changes:**
- Google search update
- TikTok/Instagram algorithm shift
- Mitigation: Diversify traffic sources

**Copyright Issues:**
- Idol name/image usage
- Mitigation: User-generated content only, clear disclaimers

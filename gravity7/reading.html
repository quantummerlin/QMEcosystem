Symbolism

Why "7 Seconds"?

7
Seconds of Stillness

The "gravity pause" myth plays with a real phenomenon: totality. During a total solar eclipse, there's a brief window — often around 2-4 minutes — where the Sun is completely hidden.

The 7 seconds represents the symbolic heart of that moment: the instant of maximum darkness before light returns. Not an ending. A held breath.

In numerology, 7 is associated with spiritual seeking, introspection, and hidden knowledge. It asks you to pause, not act.
